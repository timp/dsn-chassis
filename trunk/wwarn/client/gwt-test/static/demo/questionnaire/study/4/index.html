WWARN Study Questionnaire - Prototype 4

This page is a prototype of the study questionnaire for studies submitting data to WWARN.

The first section in the questionnaire is a set of general questions which studies applying to any WWARN module will be asked to complete.

After the general questions is a section for each of the WWARN modules.

Of these, only those sections that are relevant to the study are shown to the user.

In addition questions which depend upon answers to previous questions are displayed dynamically.
