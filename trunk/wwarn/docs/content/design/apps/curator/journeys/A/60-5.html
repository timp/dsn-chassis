Study

Title:
Uganda-Kampala 2007
Submitters:
alice@example.org bob@example.org
Summary: For this CP2 study the Faculty of Medicine, contributed samples from individuals in eight adjacent villages. Malaria transmission in the area is considered as "unstable" but endemic - it varies seasonally and is highest after the rains in the winter (EIR 0-4 ib/p/year). Of special interest for CP2 is that in this setting, infections with Plasmodium vivax are more common than infections with Plasmodium falciparum, and that severe clinical symptoms are very rare.
