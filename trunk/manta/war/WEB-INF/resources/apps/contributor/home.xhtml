Welcome to the WWARN Data Repository.

The open sharing of scientific data is recognized as a major contribution to global public health. Sharing allows data to be combined across countries and time to uncover regional and global trends, to foster scientific innovation and to improve health policy. We've carefully crafted a secure online data repository to provide researchers like you with the opportunity to contribute to an early warning system capable of identifying and preventing the spread of antimalarial resistance.

Unfinished Drafts

We recognize that sometimes you may be interrupted while registering a study. Maybe your Internet connection cuts out or you get called into a meeting. Any number of scenarios could pull you away during the registration process. Once you move ahead a step, information from the previous step will be automatically saved. You'll also see a "Save as draft" button at the bottom of each step. If you click this button it will save the information submitted to date. If you complete the registration, your study will move to "My studies". If for any reason you're interrupted, save information will be retained and made available as an unfinished draft.
